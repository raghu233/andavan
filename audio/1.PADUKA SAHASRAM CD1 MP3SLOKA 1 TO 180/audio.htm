New MP3 audio link: On 14th March, 2010, Sri Venkateswara Bhakti T.V.Channel -SVBC--webcasted an interview with H.H. Sri Ranga Ramanuja Mahadesikan, in which the interviewer put several questions on Sri Vaishnava Sampradayam and philosophy of Sri Ramanuja. H.H. has mastery in Telugu language also and replied effortlessly in fluent Telugu. Devotees who know Telugu will enjoy the discussions, but others also can understand to some extent. This is a 43 MB file, covering the whole interview of about 40 minutes. You may have to be patient while downloading from the site.As this is a treasure, we are putting this in our web site. Our thanks to Sri Veeraraghavan, Mumbai,who sent this file for our benefit.
