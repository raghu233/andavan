Srimathe Ranga RAmanuja MahAdesikAya Nama:
Srimathe SrinivAsa RAmanuja MahAdesikAya Nama:
Srimathe Vedanta RAmanuja MahAdesikAya Nama:
Srimathe RanganAtha MahAdesikAya Nama:
Srimathe SrinivAsa MahAdesikAya Nama:

A well organised & well attended ( huge attendance of shisyas, abhimanies in Sampradaya Attaire) is seen to be believed. A very big pandal was filled to capacity with thousands in attendance.This was held at Srirangam Srimath Andavan Arts & Science College Campus, Srirangam/ Thiruvanaikovil. The function was attended by many from across India, USA & many other places.

Yathis from Ahobila Mutt, Tirumala Jeer Asramam, Jeer Azwar Thirunagari, Rep Parakala Mutt graced during the 4 day function. Vidwans were also there - Sri Krishnapremi Swamin, Sri NSR Swamin, Sri Karunakarachariar, Sri Badur Rangarajan, Sri Dhamal Ramakrishnan, Asramam Sri Natteri Swamin and more

On Thirunakshtra Dinam, on 21 May 2015, Srimath Andavan was there at the Venue of Sathabhishekam much before the announced time of 7 am. Andal Goshti gave a Harathi swagatham to H H. Veda Parayanam, Divyaprabandha Parayanam filled the air, for a pleasing Morning start. Peria Satrumurai then was held with Vedam, Prabandha Pasurams recital Sambhavanai, Arulappadu. Theertham & Satari was given to every individual. Following Satrumurai, Vidwans Upanaysams, Special Postal Stamp release in honour of Srimath Andavan took place. Srimath Anadvan, Srimath Azaghiyasingar were presented a copy of Special issue .Then VIPs received the inagural Day Postal covers - Sri Ravi of The Hindu Sri Venu Srinivasan of TVS, Sri Gopalaswami Former Chief election, Commisioner.&others

Eleven books, including Sathabhisheka Malar was released by H H Srimath Andavan & Eminent Personaties received the Inaugural volumes. Present Srimath Andavan is the 11th Peetathipathi. Anugraha Bashanam was delivered by H H Srimath Andavan & H H Srimath Azaghiyasingar. Divya Desa Malai mariyadai, Abhimana Sthalam, Branch Srimath Andavan Asramam to H H Srimath Andavan was the held & it took more than two hours for his Holiness to receive these Ahugrahhams with no signs tiredness. The function concluded with Thathiaradhanam to one and all.

For over two and half hours the Sathabhishekam was telecast LIVE in Podhigai TV, and Lakhs viewed them in over 26 Countries.
