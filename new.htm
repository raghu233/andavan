31/10/2010

An important annoncement for all Sishyas of our Ashramam, particularly those residing in Villivakkam, Anna Nagar, Ambattur, Kilapuk, Shenoy Nagar and Ayanavaram areas of Chennai.

Please be informed that His Holiness Srimath Anadavan is visiting Sri Rama Anjaneyar & Sri Srinivasar Temple located in Tirumangalam Road, that is the road leading to Anna Nagar from Nathamuni Theater in Villivakkam on, Sunday, 31st October, 2010, at 4 P.M..

To brief you on this Temple ; This temple has come up nearly 18 years ago, with the great efforts of the famous Ubhaya Vedanta Vidwan and the disciple of Sri Kozhiyalam Swamy, Sri Veerapuram Krishnamachariar. He has just completed 90 years and entered his 91st year. At this age, he preferred to hand over the temple to Srimath Andavan Ashramam, as he and his family members are now sishyas of our Ashramam.

This temple is located in a very small area of half a ground of prime land. 3 tier Raja Gopuram in the front, Sri Srinivasa Perumal Moolavar and Uthsavar with Ubhaya Nachimars, Alarlemangai Thaayaar Moolavar & Uthsavar, Uthsavar Ramar, Seethai, Laksmanar, Anjaneyar Uthsavar & Moolavar, Chakrathazhwar & Nrisimhar both Uthsavar &Moolavar. These are all installed in this small temple.

As per his request, H.H. has accepted to take over this Temple to come under our Ashramam fold.

On 31st Oct,2010, at 4 PM is making His Ponnadi at the request of Sri Krishnamachariar Swamy. At that time he is handing over the signed documents to H.H. and thus formally handing over the entire Temple to Srirangam Srimath Anadavan Ashramam. From that moment, our Ashramam will administer the temple.

All our sishyas in the surrounding areas as listed above are invited to attend thye function and give a warm welcome to H.H.

For your information this temple will be developed in future with the guidance of H.H. and with the cooperation of all our sishyas,soon will be made as a landmark of our Ashramam in the  West Chennai Neighbourhood

               26th Sept. 2008 Arrived Tiruchanur Ashramam. Being Friday,that evening itself  H.H. performed Mangalasasanam of Sri Padmavathi Thaayaar Sannathi.
               27th Sept. 2008. After morning Aaraadanaas at Ashramam, H.H. again visited Thaayaar Sannathi for Managlasasanam. Towards the evening H.H reached Tirumala. .At that time TTD invited H.H. for that evening's Thomala Seva at Sri Vari Temple. H.H was given the usual Temple Honours with Perumal's Mel Saathu.At this time there was a large gathering of devotees at the Ashramam premises, from different parts of our country. Ashramam management has done elaborate arrangements for giving facilities for all the visiting devotees. Several rooms were made available in our Ashramam as well as the Mutts nearby.Sumptuous Thatheeyaaraadanams were arranged.
                28th Sept.,2008. Mahalaya Amavasyai Day. T.T D has made all arrangements for H.H. Mangalaasaasanam of Tiruvenkatamudayan Sannathi. Over 350 Sishyas of  our Ashramam have gathered in Tirumala to watch this event and have the Divyadarisanam of Perumal, along with our revered Acharyan, standing in front Kulasekaran padi.The whole event was very orderly and everyone of the ladies,gents and children , all had this very rare opportunity,which is available only once in a year. H.H was received with full Temple honours near the Aanjaneyar Sannathi and taken in a procession to the temple. After Mangalaasaasanam, H.H. was again taken round the temple in a procession, with Temple elephants leading the procession and finally reached Ashramam. Enroute, H.H. made Managlaasaasanam of Tirumalai Nambi Sannathi located on the South Maada street.
                 After this only Ashramam Theertha prasadam was received by the devotees from the Holy hands of H.H.This was again followed by special Thatheeyaaradanam.
                 At this time,TTD Chairman along with his officials arrived in Ashramam to pay respects to H.H. It was interesting to here that H.H. freely conversed with TTD Chairman in fluent Telugu. At the same time, H.H. gave a brief discourse about the importance of the forthcoming Sri Vari Brahmothsavam and this was made for TTD SVBC Bhakti TV Channel..
                 That evening H.H. reached Tiruchanur Ashramam.
     29th Oct, 2008. H.H. performed Manglasaasanam of Thaayar Sannathi, officially made by TTD.
      30th Oct. 2008.H.H. Mangalaasaasanam of Sri Govindraja Perumal sannathi
       1st Oct. 2008  H.H was invited by TTD for the lauching of Gold covered Soorya Prabhai, which is to be taken with Perumal on the Brahmothsavm day on 2nd Oct. H.H. attended that evening's function along with Beddha Jeeyar Swamy.This function was attended by A.P. Chief Minister, Sri Rajasekar Reddy and other ministers .
       2nd Oct. 2008. H.H has since returned to Chennai ashramam
 

19th August 2008

A report on Chandragrahana sanam at Tiruppallani on the night of 16th August, 2008 from Sri Raguveeradayal

Blessed we are to have the parama bhagyam of accompanying  HH Srimad Andavan at his Chandra GrahaNa snanam at Sri Adhi Sethu.


When we accompanied HH's early morning walking on 16th, HH conveyed his decision about GrahaNa snanam. We returned to the Ashramam to find, to the happiness of HH ,around 50 sevarthis, mostly from Mumbai, arrived to have AvaNi Avittam here. When they learned about HH's decision on GrahaNa Snanam they were very glad.
 

By midnight, around 01.30 we all started for Sethukkarai. On Surya GrahaNa day HH waited at the Ashramam till all the sevarthis reached seashore. But on Chandra GrahaNa day, such a parama karuNakaran HH is, he first went the Sethu before the sevarthis reached the shore,personally supervised and  arranged for satisfactory lighting arrangements with 4 gas petromax lights (as there is no light)enabling the sishyas, many of them old, to have a fearless snanam. The sankalpam was performed by Aradhakar KrishNamUrthi Maamaa.
It was a very rich anubhavam to see HH doing snanam braving poor light and very rough sea (PaurNami) and high tides. கண்டவர் விண்டிலர் நிலைதான். And all the nearly 100 sevarthis accompanied HH's Snanam at that time were in என்ன தவம் செய்தனம் என பரவசப்படும் நிலையில். HH waited at those odd hour on the sea shore till all the sishyas get into the vehicle( to ensure the safety of the sevarthis. Such was  his affection for the sishyas.
 

How great Bhagyam we all have in this Chathurmasyam! To bathe alongwith HH even once in the Adhi Sethu, ( Even Sethu Dharsnam itself will accrue innumerable PuNyam in our account totally annihilating all the sins we are determined to commit) Now we had this ahobhagyam THRICE that too within a month! Then நமக்கார் நிகர் இம்மாநிலத்தே!
 

Thus our Acharya Sarva Bauman has created history in several ways at ThiruppullaaNi! That he is the first Yathi to perform Chathurmasya Sankalpam here at Thiruppullani, is already known to members. He is the first yathi to have SEthu Snanam THRICE. Weather another Yathi will visit Thiruppullani,whether    another Yathi will be here for Sankalpam is not known. Even  if it is decided, will he perform Sankalpam here?   Will both GrahaNams happen in the same month? This situation comes very rarely.

Due to the Anugraham of Srimath Andavan, the last week was eventful. Normally on Adi Amaavaasai days the turn around of Brahmins will be very less (But on Thai Amaavaasai, Brahmins will be in thousands)But this year, the immediate presence of the Acharyan at Sethukkarai-Thiruppullani , Surya Grahanam added to that, several hundreds of Srivaishnava sevarthis turned up here and had the bhagyam of Grahana snanam at Sethukkarai with Srimath Andavan.Bruhaspathi Thiruppullani Sri Rangarajan (Nanga nallur) performed Grahana sankalpam to all assembled there. Then all the devotees had the vimochana snanam at Chakra theerththam along with Srimath Andavan. The photos are available at http://picasaweb.google.com/rajamragu/grahanasnanam and http://picasaweb.google.com/rajamragu/vimosnan

Adi Pooram, the Thiruvavathara day of Sri Andal, was being celeberated in a grand scale at Thiruppullani till several years back. Due to various reasons, for the past few years, it is limited to Ekantha thirumanjanam to Sri Andal and that's all here. Learning this, the Acharya Sarva Bauman arranged to celeberate it as was done in the past. In the morning, Jagannatha Perumal was brought to Andal Sannidhi , the Divya Thampathis had a visesha alankara thirumanjanam and veku vilakshna goshti. In the evening Perumal and Andal had a Dolothsavam in front of Thayar Sannithi. The entire expenses were borne by Srimath Andavan. These photos are available at http://picasaweb.google.com/rajamragu/adipooram

And yesterday a Rig vetha patasalai has been started here about which already reported in Yahoo groups.

Accepting the request of the Ramanathapuram Samasthanam Devasthanam, HH Srimath Andavan has ordered for a new Garuda Vahanam for Thiruppullani Temple at a cost of nearly Rupees one lakh and the work is in progress.

Thiruppullani residents , enjoying the sowlabyam of HH daily and overwhelmed by the hospitality of the Ashramam kainkaryaparals, are definitely great bhagyasalis.

25th July 2008

SRIRANGAM SRIMATH ANDAVAN'S 20TH CHATHURMASYA SANKALAPA MAHOTHSAVAM AT THIRUPPULLANI

Dear Srivaishnava perunthakaiyeer and Andavan sishyas in particular,

On behalf of the Srirangam Srimath Andavan's 20th chathurmasya sankalpa committee, we are happy to report the greatest event at Thiruppullani, namely the 20th Chathurmasya sankalpam of Srimath Andavan.

Srimath Andavan arrived at Thiruppullani on the evening of 15th July. HH was given a grand reception at the entrance of the village. The Sri Adhi Jagannaatha Perumal temple staff headed by the executive officer, Ramanathapuram Samasthanam Devasthanam, offered "STHALANGA MARIYATHAI" (Temple honours with Mangala vathyam, Perumal maalai and kaalanji will be offered to select yathis visiting this village. It is always offered to our Andavan) The 20th chathurmasya committee headed by Madurai advocate Sri T.Srinivasaraghavan and accompanied by Secretaries Thirukkurungudi Padmanaban, T.Raguveeradayal, treasurer Dindigul Sudarsan, Nanganallur Parthasarathi ,Madurai Prof. Vasudevan, Madurai LIC Rajagopalan and Vaduvoor Ganapati Govindachar offered Poorna kumbha mariyathai and Sthala abhimanis also offered poorna kumba mariyaathai. From there Srimath Andavan prefered to reach our Ashramam by walk through Sri Adhi Jagannaatha Perumal Temple and or Ashrama Desikan Sannathi. At Desikan Sannathi vAsal HH accepted the poorna kumba mariyathai and Desikan maalai. Alongwith the poorna kumbha mariyathai at the entrance offered by the committee, agraharam ladies offered muthu harathi reception. On 15th morning itself morethan 50 sishyas from various places of Tamilnadu arrived here to receive the Acharyan. As soon as HH entered the Ashramam, Vaduvoor Ganapati Sri Govindachariar and Madurai Prof. Vasudevan offered Swagatha pathrikas separately. Within few minutes Ramanathapuram Samasthanam Sethupathi Sri Kumaran Sethupathi, Madhukkur Raja and Singampatti Zamindhar came to our Ashramam and offered their prayers to HH Srimath Andavan.

On the next day morning, i.e. on Adi maasa piRappu, 300+sishyas arrived on the early morning to accompany Srimath Andavan's neeraattam at Sri Adhi Sethu Srimath Andavan was so gracious enough to wait all sishyas reach the seashore and started the maha sankalpam before the holy dip there. Everybody assembled there was in a jubilant mood. Our ashrama prthama aaraadhakar Krishnamoorthi mama performed the sankalpam followed by kshethra bruhaspathi Lakshmana Sastri. Every sishya knows the gambira thvani of our aaraathakar mama. On that day, the immediate presence of our Andavan and the big assembly of sishyas (which we have never seen before) made him more jubilant . When he recited the sankalpa manthras, even the sound of sea waves were not heard. Then Acharyan had the neeraattam at Adhi Sethu. He lead everybody upto chest deep water and had the theerthavari. All those with Andavan will ever remember that great anubhavam . Acharyan returned to Ashramam and after Padhuka Aradhanam, thatheeyaaraadhanam took place.

On 17th July, the Ramanathapuram Samasthanam Devasthanam Diwan and administrative secretary to Maharani of Ramnad himself turned up at the Ashramamwith full temple honours to invite Srimath Andavan for the mangalasasanam at Sri Adhi Jagannatha Perumal temple . At the entrance of the temple poorna kumbha mariyathai was offered. At Dwaja Sthambam, Sri Satari Mariyathai was offered by Temple Archakas. With Sri Satari leading, Our Andavan did Mangalasasanam at all Sannadhis in the Temple. Since sishyas accompanied him are large in number HH waited in each sannadhi for the sishyas to pray at the sannadhis after his mangalasasanam. Our Andavan is such a karunyamoorthi towards sishyas. While returning from the Temple, he did Mangalasasanam at Ashramam Desikan sannadhi Sri Lakshmi Hayagreeva Perumal and Swami Desikan.

On 18th, the day of Chathurmasya sankalpam the number of sishyas from all over India and a few from overseas also increased to 500+ an assembly of Srivaishnavaites this small village has not witnessed in the recent past. After Padhuka Araadhanam and theertha koshti by 8AM, Mruthsangranam by 9AM, Andavan had thirumudi vilakkam and neeraattam at the holy Chakra theertha pushkarini. It may be remembered here that our Andavan was the first yathi to bathe in this pushkarini which resulted in the pushkarini brimming with water eversince and our starvation for water is now a distant dream of the past. During this interval, our Ashrama Srikaryam veeeravalli Ganapati Desikacharyar swami, sri veeraraghavachariyar swami and Yamunachariar swami made discourses. Before delivering his Anugraha bhashanam, in tradition with our Ashramam, HH Srimath Andavan released four books on the stage. Acharya Ramamrutham part two Tamil and the same translated in English by Smt Kalyani Krishnamachari of USA, "Maniyunthu PullANiyE" (a sthala purANam of ThiruppullANi,edited and published by Shreyas ads Sri Kalyanaraman and Rangarajan brothers )and a small booklet "Visishtadvaitham" by Sri Yamunachariar. Acharyan's Anugraha bhaashanam which started with Swami Desikan's "PArAvAra " was a most scintillating one. On the day of Sankalpam, all the events were carried out EXACTLY AT THE TIMES ANNOUNCED BY OUR ACHARYAN ON THE PREVIOUS DAY. It was like a military discipline.

 
And we, the committee members, are very very happy not only because this is the FIRST Chathurmasyam at Thiruppullani but this is the first Chathurmasyam in the history when the sishyas assembled even two days before the event and enjoyed their great bhagyam to be with the Acharyan for three consecutive days. We thank those attended for their great cooperation with us. This is a small village and we made arrangements whatever is possible here. There might have been deficiencies. But, those who were here on all the three days, several of whom accustomed to very great and modern conveniences, adjusted to the situation and extended their cooperation to make this once-in-a-life event ever remembered . Their Devotion to Andavan and their Acharya Bhakthi made them determined lot to neglect all comforts and conveniences.


Our special thanks to Sri Ahobila Mutt authorities who, within a short span of three days converted the cement flooring with granite and plastering the walls with sparkling tiles so that those who came here for this special event can stay more comfortably. Their local manager Sri Muthukrishnan even cancelled his trip to Ahobilam
to take care of those staying at the Mutt. Similarly, Sri Jayarama Bhattar , in charge of Sri Vanamaamalai Mutt also offered his help for those stayed there. We thank him also. The kshethravasis also helped the visitors in several ways. "Dinamani" "Dinakaran" and "Dinamalar" papers covered the events. The chathurmasya sankalpa photos are now available at http://picasaweb.google.com/rajamragu/HH at TRPN The videos are being uploaded and links will be announced separately.
 

It is needless to say that all the abovesaid functions went off very well and without any hastles ONLY with the BLESSINGS OF our Poorvacharyars Anugrahams and the Blessings of H.H.
dasan,
 

T.Srinivasaraghavan,
