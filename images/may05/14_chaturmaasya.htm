14 th CHATUR MAASYA SANKALPAM OF H.H. SRIMATH ANDAVAN

 

1. On the 24th July, H.H. Srimad Andavan observed His 14th Chatur Maasya Sankalpam amidst an eminent gathering of Scholars, Sishyas and admirers. Our Ganapati Swami officiated as usual; over 150 Rikwiks recited the Pancha Santhi. A novel feature was that the Upanyasams of 9 Scholars was scheduled between 10.00 and 11.45 a.m. when H.H. went over to Ahobila Mutt & Sri Lakshmi Narasimhan Sannathi (after Mrith Sangrahanam) for His Mangalasasanam and other formalities such as Thirumudivilakkam, Neerattam, Maadhyanigam etc., were completed.

 

2. The gathering of sevarthis listened to these Upanyasams with rapt attention, thereafter H.H. returned to the Asramam, the program ran like a clockwork – Pancha Santhi Japam, Chatur Maasya Sankalpam, Malai Mariyadai from nearly 40 Divya Desams and Abhimana Kshetrams followed by a brief speech by yours’ truly explaining the projects of the Asramam – the progress at our Patasalas and at Srimad Andavan Arts & Science College, the road map for the Sapthathi celebrations of H.H. scheduled for May/June 2005 & the Centenary celebrations of H.H. Srimad Thirukkudanthai Andavan scheduled for Mar/Apr 2007.

 

3. H.H. thereafter delivered a short and sweet Anugraha Bashanam on Sriman NathaMuni and Sri Alavandar (24th June was the Thirunakshatram of Sri Alavandar). In the evening H.H.Sri Rangapriya Mahadesikan called on H.H. and paid His obeisance – it is a time-honoured custom for Yathis to do this if another Senior Yathi were to be present in the same place. H.H. Srimad Thirukkudanthai Andavan and H.H. used to do this every time without fail.
