A great Opportunity to this web site readers : Here is the MP3 audio rendering of the entire Srimath Valmiki Ramayanam's Sundara Kandam, done with the Thiruvaakku of our beloved Acharyan Srimushnam Andavan,Sri Ranga Ramanuja Mahadesikan. This is indeed a great effort by our Acharyan. Our request is to pay your gratitude in some way or other to our Acharyan, which we do not want to elaborate. The least you can do is to purchase these audio MP3 Cds to your self from Ashramam Book Stores. To our knowledge His Holiness Sri RangaRamanuja Mahadesikan is the only Acharyan, who has done this and it for you to enjoy hearing them
