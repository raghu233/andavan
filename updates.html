CHATURMASYA SANKALPAM STARTS ON 12-07-2014 AND ENDS ON 08-09-2014. THIS WILL BE IN SRIRANGAM PERIYASHRAMAM

25th Sanyasa Asrama Peetarohanam function of Srimushnam Andavan was celebrated well at Sripuram Andavan Ashramam in Bangalore on 26th May, 2014, in the midst of a large gathering of devotees.

This was followed by 80th Tirunakshatra function of Srimushnam Andavan and this was celebrated at Jaya Nagar Andavan Ashramam, in Bangalore on 31-5-2014. Devotees in thousands gathered on that day very enthusiastically and got the blessings of H H. Full details of this event can be seen in the forthcoming issue of Sri Ranganatha Paduka. Pictures and videos connected with these events have been posted in andavan yahoo groups, andavan facebook as well as andavan blogspot.

For the benefit of devotees, who could not be personally present on that occasion in Bangalore, Ashramam has arranged direct web casting of the whole event held on 31-5-2014. The recorded version of this is being made available for a few more weeks at http://www.chennaistream.com/andavan.
