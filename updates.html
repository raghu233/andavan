Srimath Andavan's Chaturmasya Sankalpam

Manmatha Varusha Chaturmasya Sankalpam, H H Srimad Andavan.

The current issue of Sri Ranganatha Paduka from Srimad Andavan Asramam carries the details.

H H Srimad Srimushnam Andavan Commences Chaturmasya Sanklpam ,from 31 July 2015 ,for a period of two months. Utthanam is on 28 Sep 2015.

The Chaturmasya Sankalpam is at Kanchipuram & hence H H is in this kshetram ,from 31 July to 28 Sep 2015. (on concluding day, leaves before sunrise)
