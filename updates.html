Details of Srimath Andavan's schedule of events

With the blessings of His Holiness, management committee of Srirangam Srimath Andavan Ashramam has drawn out a year long programme to celebrate Sathabishekam of Srimushnam Andavan, which is coming of in the next Vaikasi Tiruvadirai on 21st May, 2015. As per that every month on Tiruvadirai day, special functions are arranged at different Ashramam premises all over India, on a rotation basis.

NOTE ON THE SATHABHISHEKAM CELEBRATIONS OF HH SRIMAD SRIMUSHNAM ANDAVAN

VENUE – OUR SRIMAD ANDAVAN ARTS & SCIENCE COLLEGE CAMPUS IN NELSON ROAD, THIRUVANAIKOIL, TIRUCHIRAPPALLI 620005.

PARAYANAMS à The Asramam has been at the vanguard of Vedic & Saastric education for nearly 50 years now; qualified Scholars will be invited for Veda, Grantha Parayanam & will be honored with proper Sambavanai & Conveyance.

KAINKARYAPARARS have served the AchAryAs & the Institution; theirs is a labour of love performed without looking at the clock or calendar! Each one of them would earn multiples of what earn here if they were to venture outside as there is a severe dearth of qualified Adhikaris in the religious circuit all over the country! We will honour these honorable persons appropriately; the least we can do on this occasion!

SRIMAD ANDAVAN ARTS & SCIENCE COLLEGE à The College has been accredited twice with the coveted “A” Grade by the NA&AC since 2007 & has been accorded the State of Autonomy by the UGC in 2013-14 valid till AY 2019-20. The Institution is taking the next big step to become a “University”; your College is one of the eight (& the only Self Financing College) recommended by the Bharathi Dasan University to the UGC for approval as a “College of Potential Excellence”.

Full details are in the CD showing a Power point presentation on the College. HH has invested Rs. 20.00 Crores in the Physical & Rs.20.00 Crores in the Human Infrastructure for the College alone till date; we need Rs. 12-14 Crores for its immediate expansion plans. HH had applied (in the past) & will apply in the future, most of the Sambavanai for the Educational activities carried out on His Orders by Sri Ranganatha Paduka Vidyalaya Trust founded by His AchAryA, HH Srimad Thirukkudanthai Andavan.

FUTURE MISSION & PLANS à HH Srimad Andavan has defined long term Mission & Vision in the field of Traditional & Modern Education. A large number projects are to be conceived, implemented and administered and would (at current costs) entail an outlay of over Rs. 300 Crores over the next 13-15 years. As such, we are not specifically nominating any specific project to commemorate the Sathabhishekam of HH.

Cheques/DD may please be drawn in favour of “Srirangam Srimad Andavan Asramam”; those desiring the IT Exemption u/s 80-G of the IT Act, may kindly draw their Cheques/DD in favour of “Sri Paduka Charities” and/or “Sri Ranganatha Paduka Vidyalaya Trust”. While doing so, please specify where this donation be applied, say for Education, Veda Parayanam etc., Both these Trusts are recognised as “Public Charitable Trust” by the Government of India, Ministry of Finance, Department of Revenue & enjoy perpetual exemption u/s 80-G of the IT Act.

Sishyas & Abhimanis in USA may kindly make their contributions to “Sri Ramanuja Mission Inc.”, a Charitable Trust recognised by the IRS, US Federal Government. Overseas citizens of Indian Origin & NRI’s – who have taxable income in India – desiring the IT Exemption on their Indian Income u/s 80-G of the IT Act, may also kindly draw their Cheques/DD in favour of “Sri Paduka Charities”.
