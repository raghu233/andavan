Details of Srimath Andavan's schedule of events

With the blessings of His Holiness, management committee of Srirangam Srimath Andavan Ashramam has drawn out a year long programme to celebrate Sathabishekam of Srimushnam Andavan, which is coming of in the next Vaikasi Tiruvadirai on 21st May, 2015. As per that every month on Tiruvadirai day, special functions are arranged at different Ashramam premises all over India, on a rotation basis.

His Holiness is now camping in Chennai Alwarpet Ashramam. Dhanur Maasa aaraadanams are being conducted everyday early morning, besides performing Samasrayanam and Bharanyasam to the desired devotees. In Sri Srinivasa Perumal Koil located inside Chennai Ashramam, adhyayana uthsavam has started with Pagal paththu is under way, which is to be followed by Raappathu starting from Vaikunta Ekadasi day on 1st January, 2015. In connection with the forthcoming Sathabisheka Mahothsavam of Srimath Andavan, monthly aaraadanams on each Tiruvadirai days are under way at different locasions of Ashramam, as per the schedule noted below in the chart.

Below is the schedule of activities, as information to all of you. Ashramam invites devotees to take note of the dates and attend the celebrations at any of those places, as per their convenience. The most important event will be held in Srirangam Periashramam on Vaikasi Tiruvadirai day on 21st May, 2015. Please make sure to be present there, a couple of days before itself and enjoy participating in the momentous event. Plan to make travel bookings as well as for your stay. Ashramam will come out at the appropriate time the list places, where accomodation can be provided . In addition a list of lodges can be identified, their addresses and contact numbers can be prepared and circulated. Our National Committee Chairman Shri RR will be working on these and will be communicating to you all.
