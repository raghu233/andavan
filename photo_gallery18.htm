A new Ashramam property has come up at Zahirabad,about 100 KMS from Hyderabad,at the borders of 3 states of AP,karnataka and Maharashtra.This was made possible by the untiring efforts Hyderabad Committee Chairman,Sri K.Saranyan,Secretary Sri Chitra Rajagopalan and Member Sri Rangachariar
This site is identified for developing into an Education &Research Centre as per the wishes of His Holiness The picture shows the visit of H.H. to the site along with the Committee members.
On 5th October 2004 His Holiness visited the site and blessed the site with His Ponnadi and this site will definitely grow with the blessings of Divya Dhampathees and Sri Padukas of Poorvachariars
