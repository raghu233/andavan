E- MAGAZINE OF SRI RANGANATHA PADUKA

We are pleased to inform you that we have started an initiative of publishing the widely read "Sri Ranganatha Paduka" magazine electronically.

It is our intention to make this e-magazine available in our Ashramam Website www.andavan.org under the Publications tab initially on a trial basis

The pilot of this magazine is available for trying out in the opening page as below

Click here for E-Magazine of Sri Ranganatha Paduka

Being a pilot run, the look and feel of the magazine will be in plain black and white "scroll-through' style.

However on full implementation, it will be identical to what you receive in print, and we assure you that you will have an even better feel than reading in print.

We request you to go through and provide your valuable feedback to r_sv@yahoo.com

Our proposal is to make this e-magazine available to those who subscribe on-line. The details of this will be made known at a later date.

The main advantage will be for overseas subscribers of the magazine, to reduce the burden of high cost of airmail postage from India.

Daasan,
