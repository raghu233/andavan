MahaSamprokshanam of Sri Santhana Venugopala Swamy(2004)

This is a vadakalai sampradaya temple,where Swamy Desikan is also installed,which is a rare one in this part of the State.This must be a must visit for all Srivaishnavaites,to see for themselves the beautifully sculptured moolavar Sri Venugopalan,with a cow, licking his bottom of his right foot.Srimath Andavan took special interest in Samprokshanam of this long neglected temple.We will be glad to receive a feedback from those who visit this temple after seeing this message in this web site.Post your comments to r_sv@yahoo.com
The Moolavar is a very rare statute, Sri Santhana Venu Gopala swamy with a Cow beautifully sculptured in one Single Stone along with Rukmini, Sathyabhama on either
side.
This is a very ancient temple with Vadakalai Sampradhayam and Vaiganasa Agamam
Sri Santhana Venugopalan Swamy Temple at place called Sri Venugopala Puram located on the Thiruttani - Sholinghur road at about 10 KM from Sholinghur
