Trusts Associated with  Andavan Ashramam

1.Sri Paduka Charities, NO.33,Desikachari Road,Mylapore Chennai 600040., Contact phone 044 2499 3658

   This is the Apex trust connected with all the activities of Ashramam.This Trust supports all the major activities of our Ashramam towards the well being of our Society at large,propagation of Vedic studies,publications of vedic literatures,honouring of ubaya vedanthic scholars ,to mention a few.This is a registered Trust and donations made to this Trust are having the benefit of income tax exemption under 80G.This trust maintains a KalyanaMandapam in Chennai and also the Veda Patasala at Thiruvaheendrapuram.This trusts also conducts once every year "Samashti Upanayanam" in the month of Maasi,to atleast 35 poor brahmin boys of 3 sects,Vaishnava,Smartha and Madwa. Entire expenses are met by this trust. Trust deed permits transfer of funds to Ashramam for specified activities as permitted by  Government rules.

4.Sri Srimath Bhagavath Bhashyakara Thathiyaradanai Trust.,Address: Manager, Srirangam Srimath Andavan Ashramam, Ring Road, Tirumala Pin 517504, contact phone number 0877 2277826

Main objectives of this Trust is to organise Perumal and Paduka Aaraadanam everyday, provide thadheeyaaradanam  to Sevarthees visiting Tirumala free of cost, provide free food packets to the needy people in Tirumala , provide free dormitory accomodation with free locker facility to the needy visiting pilgrims in Tirumala ,organise veda parayanam and divyaprabanda parayanam whenever required, particularly during Brahmothsavam periods on a large scale. Funds are mobilised  through donations from public, which has the benefit Income tax exemption under 80G. It also generates additional revenue by providing individual room accomodations in the upper floors of Ashramam
