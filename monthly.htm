28th July 2003

Report on Chaturmasya Sankalpam of Srimath Andavan Swamigal

A  Brief report on Chaturmasya Sankalpam  celebrations at Alwarthirunagari of Srimath Andavan Swamigal. 18th July 2003 was a great day in the history of Srirangam Srimath Andavan Ashramam at Alwarthirunagari, the sacred place of Sri Nammalwar, our Ashramam has its own residential property.  A couple of old houses on the outer street of Sri Aadhinaathar Temple were bought and with the support of TVS family, a fully renovated building has been consecrated by  His Holiness to accommodate both Srimath Andavan Ashramam and Sri Ranganatha Paduka Vidyalaya. Devotees from all over India, including a large contingent from Mumbai, started arriving one day before itself to witness the commencement of Chatumasya Sankalpam of His Holiness on 18th July.  The renovated buildings with all the facilities have come out very nicely, thanks to the coordination efforts done by our NEC Chairman Sri R Rajagopal and the efforts of a dedicated team workers deputed by T.V.S., who worked day and night for the last 2 months to complete the facility to be ready for occupation well before the commencement of Sankalpam. Opposite to our Ashramam is the famous  Sri Aadhinaathar Temple, within which lies the famous "Urangaapuli" tamarind tree, which was the abode of Sri Nammalwar and from where the famous Thiruvoimozhi paasurams originated.  This tree continues to exist even today from the time Nammalvar around 5000 years ago.

Behind our Ashramam is Tambirabarani River, which is the lifeline of the entire Tirunelveli District. In these pleasant surroundings our Ashramam started functioning at Alwarthirunagari, which is central to the famous Nava Thirupathi Divyadesams.

From the early morning itself on the 18th July, hundreds of devotees started assembling at Ashramam.  Sri Karyam Swamy, Sri Narasimharaghavachariar,  Sri R.Rajagopal,.Sri Vaduvoor Srinivasa Desikachariar, Sri Kalyanapuram Aravamudachariar, Sri Villoor Karunakarachariar were all in the center stage in organizing all matters connected to the event.From Sri Paduka Charities,Sri S.Vasudevan, Secretary, Sri Sarangarajan,Sri Kasturirangan, Sri Krishnan,Sri Parthasarathi,R.Srinivasan and Sri V.Srinivasan were in attenadance. (Sri Narayanan of Srimath Andavan Trust, a native of adjacent Keezhnaththam,was also present.

After the usual mornings anushtanam,  Srimath Andavan performed Perumal and Paduka Aaradanams.  Sri Venugopalan araadanam was performed by aaraadakar Sri Calcutta Krishnamurthy swamy.  After this it was theertha ghoshti for the devotees.

Next event was Mrithsankramanam, the collection of earth by Srimath Andavan, Sri Vaduvoor Desikahariar swamy did the sankalpam ceremony for HH.  All the devotees attentively participated in this even and considered the same as a great blessings in their lifetime.

On completion of this, H.H, went for "Thirumudivilakkam" on the banks of river Tambirabarani.  After taking bath at the river and  "Thirumankappu" .  H.H. returned back to Ashramam in a procession and at the entrance H.H., was received with Mangala Aarathi by the ladies present there.

During this period, several vidwans present in the Hall, gave discourses for the devotees.  Several other devotees went to the nearby Divyadesa Temple of Sri Adinathar and the sannathi of Sri Nammalwar.

All these events were followed by Pancha Santhi by vedic scholars and Divyadesa mariyaadais for H.H., Among them were Srivilliputhur, Kancheepuram, Thirukkudanthai, Uppiliappan Koil, Thiruvananthapuram, all the nine divyadesams of Nava Thiruppathi, Vanamaamalai dand Thirukkurungudi etc.,  Srikaryam Sri Narasimha Raghavachariar Swamy presented all these temple honours to H.H

After this H.H. delivered His anugrahabashanam. At the end of these,  a grand Thadeeyaaraadanam was arranged for all the vedic scholars and devotees. On conclusion of all these events, H.H., gave manthratchadai to all the devotees and blessed them.

Thus concluded the first day of Chaturmasya Sankalpam. The devotees who attended this function, had a great opportunity to visit all the 9 divyadesams of Nava Thirupathi and the adjacent Vaanamaamalai and Thirukkurungudi. 

The devotees had the opportunity of visiting Nava Thirupathi.  The credit of renovation and excellent maintenance of all of them goes to the big three letter word T.V.S., and particularly under the giant Industrialist and grand son of TVS, Sri Venu Srinivasan.  Every one in those villages speaks very high of TVS.  It is a great sight to see the new vibrant Nava Thirupathees now.  Every where spic and span, discipline and right as per time schedule. Those who have not visited these kshetras and may now take this great opportunity provided by H.H., who is now camping there for two months.  You can get blessings of Srimath Andavan Swamigal, as well as visit the Divyadesams.  No worry about food, there is Andavan Ashramam for you there.  Nearer to this there are 2 other divyadesams of Thirukkurungudi and Vaanamaamalai.  At Thirukkurungudi apart from the well known five Nambi temples, please do visit Parivattapparai of Sri Ramanuja and Brindavanam of Sri Thirumangai Azhwar.

You can take this opportunity to pay respects  Srimath Vaanamaamalai Jeer Swamy and Srimath Thirukkurungudi Jeer Swamy also.  In short, three Vaishnavaite Yatheewaras are observing their Chaturmasya Sankalpam on the banks of river Tambirabarani and it will be our bhaagyam to get their blessings.

Nearer to Srivaikuntam there is a famous Sri Venkateswara  Temple atop a small hill, in a place called Karungulam,visited as a prarthanai sthalam by many devotees.

Daasan,

I have the privilege of submitting the following report to the Honourable Members since my previous report sent a few months ago.

 14th CHATUR MAASYA SANKALPAM OF H.H. SRIMAD ANDAVAN 

As already reported this was observed by H.H. Srimad Andavan at our Sripuram Asramam from July 24th onwards. H.H. had a well-earned rest for nearly eight weeks due to the restrictions on His movements during the Sankalpam period. As usual H.H. immersed Himself in His books, (existing stocks and newly procured!) had Chintana with great Scholars on certain core issues of our Sampradayam.

The Trustees/ Staff/ Committee Members of our Sripuram Asramam had made excellent arrangements for the Sankalpam, so H.H. had a very comfortable stay for these eight weeks, many Sishyas and Abhimanis had the Bhagyam of His Darshan; many Sishyas had Pancha Samskaram and Atma Samarpanam (Saranagathi) during this period. There were Tele Upanyasams on the series “Sthothra Rathnam” of Srimad YAmunamuni to our overseas audience. 

H.H. celebrated the Thirunakshatrams of His Poorvacharyas, their Holinesses Srimad Vazhuthoor Andavan, Srimad Therazhundur Andavan, Srimad Akkur Andavan, Srimad Thenbirai Andavan and Srimad Mysore Andavan at the Sripuram Asramam with Veda/ Prabandha/ Grantha Parayanams. On 16/09/2002, H.H. inaugurated and blessed the project at Jayanagar Asramam for the boys’ hostel therein. The same day the Maha Samprokshanam of the Rajagopuram of Sri Srinivasa Perumal Temple at Rajaji Nagar was performed in the immediate presence of H.H. Srimad Andavan. On the 21st September H.H. performed Mangalasasanam at Sri Lakshmi Narasimhan Sannathi at the Ahobila Mutt at Prakash Nagar.  

The Sripuram Asramam gave an unprecedented & very well deserved Sambavanai to all the Kainkaryaparas of H.H. at the end of the Sankalpam. This was a matter of extreme satisfaction to the Staff and the National Committee. It is hoped that the other Centres match or better this in the years to come. These Kainkaryaparas are our unsung warriors, there is a wrong impression due to their noise levels and they are sometimes difficult to handle. But the job requirements are very strenuous, suitable hands are difficult to recruit, most people go for Upadhyayam or for Cooking in marriages/ other functions where there is very good money to be earned. Under the circumstances, (taking an overall view and leaving out a couple of black sheep who have been sacked anyway) the their sincere services rendered are to be appreciated and well rewarded. I am very happy that the Sripuram Asramam Committee has done so and would like to record my appreciation thereof. I appeal to all centres to replicate this whenever H.H. Srimad Andavan camps at their centres.

H.H. performed His Uthanam on 22nd September at Sri S. Ravi Narayanan’s residence at Jayanagar. The two brothers Badri and Ravi are rendering yeoman services while maintaining a very low profile. Just one word from H.H. is the veritable Geetha Baashyam for them both. I am happy to note their commitment and interest, which will guarantee the future welfare of the Institution, as they are young in their early forties.

MAASI PUSHYAM 2003 – SECUNDERABAD ASRAMAM SILVER JUBILEE

Our Secunderabad Asramam was established in the year 1978 by our Poorvacharya, the late H.H. Srimad Thirukkudanthai Andavan who consecrated the Sannathi for Maha Lakshmi Thayar and Sri Lakshmi Narayana Perumal to cater to the Sevarthis in the Twin cities. This Asramam has been making spectacular progress over the past 25 years with the addition of Kalyana Mandapam and due to the flocking of large number of Sevarthis without differentiation of caste and creed. The Aradhanams and the daily rituals, the Utsavams are conducted with total religious fervour and commitment by the Aradhakar duly supported by the Committee members from the beginning. As per the Niyamanam of H.H. Srimad Andavan, the Committee has been converted into a Trust last year. The Trust Board is headed by Sri K. Saranyan, I.P.S. (Retd.). The other Trustees are M/S A.R. Rangachari (Vice President), Chithra R. Rajagopalan (Secretary), C.R. Sridhar, S. Venugopalan and Krishnam Raju.

The Trustees requested that the Silver Jubilee celebrations be conducted in a simple and austere manner with appropriate Homams being performed in the benign presence of H.H. Srimad Andavan. Accordingly H.H’s Yatra program was fixed up and He reached the Twin cities in time for the celebrations. Our Asthana Ganapati Sri Veeravalli Srinivasa Desikachariar and his (Super band of Adhikaris) assistants then performed Purusha Sookta/ Sree Sookta/ Sudarsana/ Sri Lakshmi Narasimha/ Dhanvanthri/ Sammanana/ Sada Rudriya Vasordhara Homams on the 12th and 13th March 2003. It was a super sublime experience for those of us fortunate enough to see the Homams being performed with tremendous care, professionalism and reverence. Hundreds of Sevarthis sat through and watched the virtuoso performance on these two days by a Master who is revered in the World of Vedas as an Ultimate Authority, in the presence of our Acharya SArvabhouma. 

There were Veda/ Prabandha/ Grantha Parayanams from the 11th March for the 203rd Thirunakshatram celebrations of our Poorvacharya, H.H. Srimad PeriAndavan. The total cost of the project is estimated @ Rs. 80.00/ 85.00 lakhs. We have so far spent Rs. 30.00 lakhs from our own funds; After obtaining the permission of H.H. we are taking a loan of Rs. 50.00 lakhs @ 9.75% interest p.a. to be repaid in say 65/70 installments of Rs. 1.00 lakh per month. The assistance of Sri N.S. VaradAchAri, Advocate (& our Sishya and Trustee Sri Kasturi Rangan) is to be appreciated in this regard. This will be repaid from out of the incomes of the Thirumala Asramam and the new Kalyana Mandapam, which will generate an income of approx. Rs. 1.00/ 1.25 lakh per month.

The scheduled completion of the Asramam/ Veda Patasala building is mid April 2003; we are having the Griha Pravesam on Monday the 28th April 2003 between 04 30 and 05 30 hours; all are invited to attend and grace the occasion. TWIN CITIES – THE SECOND ASRAMAM

Very soon, with the Divya Niyamanam of H.H., we shall be acquiring another plot to build a second Asramam in the Twin cities. The Asramam in West Marredpally is ideally and centrally located but reels under a severe (well) water shortage! Asramam has this unbending policy of conducting the Aradhanams/ Thirumanjanam/ Thaligai etc., with well water (& drawn by hand) only!! It is a classic case of the irresistible force and the immovable object – something has got to give! 

At the same time, the devotion of the all classes of Sishyas and Abhimanis in the Twin cities is so sublime that we cannot deprive them of the Bhagyam of the visits and blessings of the Acharya just because of the well water problem. Thus we have to solve the problem by moving out to a location where we can get well water, build another Asramam, ensure that H.H. visits the Twin cities regularly and enable the Sevarthis therein to receive the boundless Blessings of H.H. Srimad Andavan. 

This project will be taken up on priority. With the Poorvacharyas’ Blessings, we hope that the next visit of H.H. will be to the new Asramam. 12-06-2002 Update

Venue: Srirangam Srimath Andavan Ashramam

Mylapore, Chennai.

The 68th Thirunakshathra Celebrations of His Holiness Sriranga Ramanuja Mahadesikan was celebrated in a grand scale at Mylapore Ashramam today.

Sri Parakala Mutt Jeer Swamy arrived at the Ashramam premises, two days before to grace the occasion, and was accorded a warm reception, on his arrival. Presence of Parakala Mutt Jeer Swamy was among the highlights of this function.

His Holiness Sri Ranga Ramanuja Mahadesikan arrived at Ashramam Premises, earlier after his Mangalaasasanam

at Tirumala Temple last month. Every day large number of devotees attended the Aaradhanams and obtained the Blessings of His Holiness.

The Thirunakshathram functions started on 8th June and with Parayanams of Vedas and Prabhandams every day. Everyday evening Carnatic Music Performances were also arranged.

Aaradhanams of Perumal and Padukas were performed by His Holiness everyday in the morning, and Theertha Prasadams were issued to the devotees. Sri Parakala Mutt Jeer Swamy also performed Aaradhanams of Sri Lakshmi Hayagreeva Perumal at the same time. It was a grand sight to see the Two Acharyas Performing Aaradhanams at the same time side by side and it was a very grand occasion on these days for the devotees to have the Bhagyam like this.

Todays function of Thirunakshathram was attended by more than 3000 devotees, from all over India. Many Ubaya Vedantha Vidwans graced the Occasion and the photographs of this occasion are attached with this.

Srimath Andavan Ashramam released several publications to coincide with this function and one of the books was Chathusloki, based on the tele upanyasams delivered by His Holiness earlier.

The audio cassettes on Chathusloki as well as Dhaya sathakam was also released, as Rendered by His Holiness.

His Holiness honoured Sri U.Ve. Vaduvoor Srinivasa Desikachariar Swamy and also Sri U.Ve. Payyampadi Srivatsangachariar Swamy in appreciation of their award conferred on them by the President of India, for being outstanding sanskrit scholars. His Holiness also honoured, Sri Madurai Krishnan with a title from the Ashramam, for his many years of service to Carnatic Music. Sri U.Ve. Ramadesikachariar was also specially honoured by His Holiness.

The whole function started in the early morning, and Veda, Prabhandha Saathumarais were held. Both Srimath Andavan and Sri Parakala Mutt Jeer gave Theertha Prasadams, to the Devotees assembled. After the Saathumarai, Divya Desa Maryadhais, from over 30 Temples were given to both the Swamigals. After brief discourses by several Ubaya Vedantha Vidwans, Sri Parakala Mutt Jeer Swamy spoke on the occasion in praise of Srimath Andavan Swamigal and wished him many more years of service to Srivaishnavism including Sapthapathim, Sathabhishekam, and Sathamaanam.

The function concluded with Srimath Andavan's Anugraha Bhaashanam with a heart rendering Slokam in praise of Srimath Periandavan, with a reference to Swami Dattathreya.

Devotees were given Thadheeyaradhanam at the conclusion of the occasion.

A full report on this will appear in the forthcoming issue of Sri Ranganatha Paduka Magazine. This report is made first hand to inform the readers of the Srimath Andavan Website, at the earliest.
